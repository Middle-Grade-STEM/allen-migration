Basic Information About Migration:

Migration is a common activity that involves the movement of people from one place to another. Humans have always migrated, and still migrate today. There are many causes of migration. People may migrate to a specific place because they choose to. Some examples of this are moving to somewhere for a better job, or because of the change of the season. People may also be forced out of a place and must move to somewhere else. Some examples of people being forced out of a country include persecution, war, natural disasters, and more. People will keep moving as long as they live on Earth.
